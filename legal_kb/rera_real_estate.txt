# RERA and Real Estate

## Overview
The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act improves transparency and accountability in real-estate projects by regulating developers and protecting homebuyers.

## Applicable Acts
- RERA Act, 2016

- Rights and obligations

## Common Clauses

- Builder Obligations
- Buyer Rights
- Possession

- Compensation

## Practical Guidance
- Identify parties and responsibilities.
- Review timelines and deadlines.
- Verify financial obligations.
- Check governing law and dispute resolution.
- Ensure signatures and execution requirements are complete.

## Risk Indicators
- Unregistered projects
- Delayed possession
- Hidden charges
- Incomplete disclosures
