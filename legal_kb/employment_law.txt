# Employment Law

## Overview
Employment law regulates the relationship between employers and employees, including hiring, wages, leave, workplace conduct, confidentiality, and termination.

## Applicable Acts
- Industrial Relations Code, 2020
- Code on Wages, 2019
- Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code, 2020

## Key Concepts
- Purpose and scope
- Rights and obligations
- Common terminology
- Practical interpretation
- Compliance considerations

## Common Clauses
- Notice Period
- Probation
- Leave
- Confidentiality
- Non-solicitation
- Termination

## Risk Indicators
- Unclear job duties
- Missing notice period
- Broad restrictive covenants
- No grievance mechanism

## Practical Review Checklist
- Identify the parties.
- Verify dates and duration.
- Review financial obligations.
- Examine termination conditions.
- Check governing law and dispute resolution.
- Review confidentiality and liability.
- Confirm signatures and execution requirements.


## Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What should be reviewed first?
A: Parties, obligations, payment terms, duration, and termination.

Q: What makes a clause risky?
A: Ambiguous language, unlimited liability, unilateral rights, or missing remedies.

Q: Should AI replace legal advice?
A: No. AI can summarize and highlight issues but should not replace professional legal advice.
